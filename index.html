The ASSET (Automated Systems SEcuriTy) Research Group at the Singapore University of Technology and Design does focused research to enahnace the safety, security and efficiency of Hardware/Software Systems. Here is a place where we tell you exactly what that means! We are always looking for researchers at all levels (Bachelor, Masters, PhD) to enrich the scientific activities in the group. For Post-doc positions, take a look at the individual research projects for opening. If you wish to join our group, contact Sudipta Chattopadhyay email: sudipta_chattopadhyay@sutd.edu.sg directly with your CV.
